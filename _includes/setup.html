Overview

Requirements

Integrating your cluster into OSG has fairly low requirements. Your cluster should meet the following requirements:

  • Slurm, SGE, PBS, LSF, or HTCondor job scheduler
  • Submit node should be accessible using ssh
  • Compute nodes should have internet connectivity (NAT is fine)
  • Compute nodes should use RHEL/Centos/Scientific Linux 6 or 7
