Overview

Requirements

Integrating your cluster into OSG has fairly low requirements. The OSG software works with a variety of job schedulers (Slurm, SGE, LSF, PBS, HTCondor). The only hard requirements are that there must be a submit node that can be accessed through ssh and that compute nodes have internet connectivity (NAT is fine).
